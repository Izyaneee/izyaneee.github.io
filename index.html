Video Embedding Tutorial

How to Embed a Service-Hosted Video

Step 1: Open Your Video

Go to your video on the hosting service and find the embed option.

Step 2: Copy the Embed Code

Copy the provided embed code from the hosting service.

Step 3: Paste the Code in Your HTML

Insert the copied code into your HTML where you want the video to appear.
